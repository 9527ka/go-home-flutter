## Issue 2: Guideline 2.3.6 - Age Rating (In-App Controls)

**Apple's Feedback:**
The Age Rating indicates "In-App Controls" but no Parental Controls or Age Assurance mechanisms were found.

**Our Explanation:**

We sincerely apologize for the confusion regarding the Age Rating selection.

**About Our App:**
"回家了么" (Go Home) is a **public welfare platform for finding missing persons**. Main features include:
- Publishing missing person notices
- Submitting clues and tips
- Real-time chat communication
- Viewing missing person posts

**Why We Incorrectly Selected "In-App Controls":**

We misunderstood the definition of "In-App Controls" when filling out the Age Rating questionnaire. Our app:
- Does **NOT** contain parental control features
- Does **NOT** include age verification mechanisms
- Does **NOT** target children as the user base
- Is designed to help **adults find missing persons** (including missing children), but the app users are adults

**Corrected:**
- Updated "In-App Controls" to **"None"** in App Store Connect

## Issue 3: Guideline 2.1 - Information Needed (Law Enforcement Partnership)

**Apple's Question:**
Have you partnered with any local law enforcement?

**Our Answer:**

**Current Status:**
We currently have **NOT** established formal partnerships with any law enforcement agencies.

**Nature of Our App:**
- "回家了么" (Go Home) is a **civil public welfare platform** designed to help families publish missing person information and collect leads
- Our role is to serve as an **information publishing and communication bridge**, not to conduct law enforcement or investigations
- All information posted by users is voluntarily provided; we do not verify identities or conduct case investigations

**Test Data Source:**
- The current test data in the app is sourced from the "110 Missing Persons Network" (110寻亲网) platform
- All information represents **real and valid missing person cases**
- We collect publicly available information to demonstrate platform functionality

**Content Moderation Mechanism:**
- All missing person notices posted by users are reviewed by administrators
- We filter inappropriate content, false information, and spam
- For content suspected of illegal activity, we promptly remove it and keep records

**Commitment:**
- Willing to establish cooperation mechanisms with law enforcement agencies
- Will cooperate with any lawful information requests and case investigations
- Will comply with all legal and regulatory requirements in our operating regions
